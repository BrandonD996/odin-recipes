Marone Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Meat
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Cook Mean in Pan until brown
  2. Boil Noodles until Al Dente
  3. Place layer of sauce in bottom of pan
  4. Place layer of noodle and meat
  5. Layer sauce, noodle, and meat until pan is full
  6. Bake for 15 Minutes
  7. Remove pan from oven and spread cheese across the top
  8. Melt cheese in oven (about 4 minutes)
  9. Let cool and enjoy!
